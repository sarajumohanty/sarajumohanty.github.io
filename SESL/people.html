Faculty Members

Prof. Saraju P. Mohanty
(Director)
Professor,
Department of Computer Science and Engineering 		Dr. Mohanty obtained Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from the University of South Florida. His research interests are the following:
  1. Security and Energy Aware Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS)
  2. IoT-based Approaches for Smart Healthcare and Smart Agriculture
  3. IoT-enabled Consumer Electronics for Smart Cities and Smart Villages

Prof. Elias Kougianos
 Professor,
Department of Electrical Engineering 		Dr. Kougianos obtained Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from the Louisiana State University. He has extensive industrial experience in semiconductor process integration, process and device simulation, and VLSI design. His current research interests include analog and mixed signal circuit simulation and the application of stochastic techniques to the solution of electromagnetic problems.
