Lab Policy

Course Homepage : http://www.csee.usf.edu/~smohanty/teaching/ComputerSystemDesignFall2003

  • Students must read the lab and complete any prelab work prior to the lab session.

  • Attendance of the lab is mandatory. Deductions from lab grades may be made for unapproved absences, late arrivals, and early leaving.

  • All students must have their completed lab verified by the supervising TA in order to receive a grade.

  • Lab reports are due by the end of the lab period of the lab due date and must be submitted to the supervising TA.

  • Unless prior consent is given by the supervising TA, a late penalty of a letter grade will be deducted for each day from the due date that a lab assignment (verification or report) is late.

  • The lab will follow the honor policy for the course. Cheating will not be tolerated.
